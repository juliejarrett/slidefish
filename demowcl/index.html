How it Works

1. Schedule your pick-up

Choose the most convenient two hour window between 4pm-8pm. Here's a map of all the areas we servce. Questions or changes to your appointment? Call us at 1-888-611-WASH (9274).

2. Give us your dirty laundry

No amount is too much! Have your laundry ready in a container or trash bag, and we'll pedal over and pick it up. We'd be delighted to make your acquaintence in person, or you can just leave your laundry with your doorman, on your porch, or in your secret hiding place.

3. Get it back in 24 hours

We use high-efficiency machines and Sun and Earth Brand hypoallergenic detergents to get your laundry clean as new. Then we carefully fold it and return it during your chosen time window in one of our snazzy green bags.

Our Impact

Pedal Power > Diesel Trucks

Some think that our bike trailers are "cute." We love the compliments, but we don't think "cute" is quite the right word for the rigs that hauled a million pounds of cargo through three Philadelphia winters, with just about zero greenhouse emissions (outside of breathing). Moving freight around cities accounts for nearly a quarter of transportation-sector carbon emissions, and we're proving that there's a greener, cheaper alternative.

Saying "No" to Dirty Laundry

Contrary to what you might think, cleaning your clothes can be dirty business between all the detergents, water, and energy involved. That's why we use locally-made, hypoallergenic, natural Sun and Earth brand detergents, high-efficiency machines, and a whole lot of care to keep everything squeaky clean.

We Recycle Your Money, Too

Using bikes is about more than the environment. When you use Wash Cycle Laundry, more of your money gets paid out to Philadelphians as wages and recycled in our community rather than sent off as a loan or insurance payment on a truck built somewhere else.

Laundry as a Launching Pad

Wash Cycle Laundry partners with Philadelphia Works and Gearing Up, two amazing organizations that help us hire driven, motivated adults ready to get back on their feet. You can read and watch more about how Wash Cycle helps your neighbors thrive at work.
